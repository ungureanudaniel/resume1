Hi! I am Daniel Ungureanu — a passionate Python developer and GIS technician with an academic background in forestry and forest ecology. I bring hands-on experience working at the intersection of programming, ecology, biodiversity monitoring and geospatial technology, driven by a deep interest in understanding and protecting forest ecosystems.
