Hi! My name is Daniel Ungureanu. I am a Forest Engineer, with a M.Sc. in Forest Ecology, GIS enthusiast, junior python developer and nature lover! I have a strong passion for tech, ~8 years in nature related projects, working constantly with QGIS, ARCGIS, MySQL and PostgreSQL databases, ~3 years in web development with Python, Django, SQL and more, as a freelancer. My aim for the future is to go deeper into programming & GIS, learn new skills, by getting involved in complex & challenging projects.
